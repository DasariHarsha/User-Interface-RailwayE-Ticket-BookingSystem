• In order to have successful travel insurance policy please update your user profile without special character in User Name, Pin Code, Mobile Number and Email Id in your IRCTC user profile.

  • Passengers are advised to travel with the screen shot of ERS i.e. Virtual Reservation Message (VRM), SMS received from IRCTC along with any one of the prescribed identity card in original. Please save paper and
    environment and do not print the ERS unless it is extremely necessary to do so.

  • IRCTC does not ask your credentials/bank OTP/password by any means. Please do not share it with anyone.

  • Loyalty Program NGeT: If you are facing issue during login, then kindly try to reset your password using Forgot Password

  • Ticket booking through Internet is allowed from 00:20 Hrs to 23:45 Hrs.

  • If your pnr is not generated and payment is deducted at bank, the same will be refunded back in 3-4 working days.

  • Users are requested to delete browser cookies/cache if facing login problems or logout issue.

  • Concession based Internet ticketing for physically challenged persons is available using the Photo Identity Cards issued by Indian Railways.

  • Multiple logins at www.irctc.co.in are not allowed.

  • In case of partial cancellation, diversion or short termination of trains, E-Ticket passengers are required to file TDR as per refund rules to claim the refund.

  • Internet booking is not permitted in the overseas train nos 14001,14002,14889,13108 & 13109.

  • E-ticket confirmed through charting at originating station or at the previous remote location cannot be cancelled. The refund of such ticket will be processed through TDR

  • Never purchase e-ticket from unauthorized agents or persons using their personal IDs for commercial purposes.Such tickets are liable to be cancelled and forfeited without any refund of money, under section (143) of the
    Indian Railway Act 1989. List of authorized agents are available on www.irctc.com E-Ticket Agent Locator.

  • SMS delivery is subject to effective transmission from your mobile service operator and mobile carrier network availability. In case, you have not received Booking SMS, Please check “Booked Ticket History” before
    proceeding for new ticket booking.
